Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles uncooked
  • 4 cups mozzarella cheese shredded and divided
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese shredded and divided
  • Tomato sauce
  • Beef, sausage, onion, garlic mix
  • 2 cups of ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley chopped
  • 1 egg beaten

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit. Cook pasta and set aside.
  2. Brown the meat, garlic, and onion mix
  3. Stir in the tomato sauce and simmer for 5 minutes
  4. Mix the cheeses together
  5. Add the meast sauce to pan
  6. Top with 3 lasagna noodles. Layer with 1/3 of the cheese mix and meat sauce.
  7. Repeat twice more
  8. Finish with 3 noodles topped with remaining sauce
  9. Cover with foil and bake 45 minutes
  10. Uncover, sprinkle with remaining cheese and bake for 15 more minutes
  11. Rest 10-15 minutes before cutting
